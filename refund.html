Return & Refund Policy

At Technotronics Ltd, we stand behind our laser detection technology. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Return & Refund Policy We want our customers to be satisfied with their purchase. If you are not happy with your order, please review the policy below. Eligibility Returns are accepted within 14days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resellable condition. Non-Returnable Items Custom or personalized items, damaged goods due to incorrect use, or products specified as non-returnable are not eligible. Refund Process Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of approval or denial. Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method within a standard processing period. Return Shipping Unless the item was defective or sent in error, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Damaged or Incorrect Items If you receive a defective or wrong product, contact us immediately with photos for quick replacement or refund. For return or refund requests, contact our support team.
